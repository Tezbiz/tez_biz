Proprietorship can be set up for the Proprietor to do business on small scale. Proprietorships are ideal for very small businesses in the unorganised sector with only one Promoter. Tej offers proprietorship registration from Rs.2899/-

What is Proprietorship ?

Sole Proprietor as its name suggest requires only one natural person who can own, manage and control the whole of the affairs. Sole Proprietor is one of the most popular form of business in India. Sole proprietorship's are being used by micro and small businesses operating in the unstructured sectors. It can be started vey easily and quickly with very minimum regulatory compliances. However, the Proprietorship after start up does not provide the benefits such as limited liability, corporate status, separate legal entity, independent existence, transferability, perpetual existence etc.- which are important features for any business.

In India, the Proprietorship can not be registered under any of the laws. However, GST Registration, Shops and Establishment Registration, Professional Tax registration can be obtained in the name of the Proprietor to establish that the Proprietor is operating a business as a sole proprietorship. Thus, all the registrations for a proprietorship would be in the name of the Proprietor and therefore the Proprietor is personally liable for all the liabilities of the Proprietorship. Therefore, now-a-days the people can also choose to incorporate the One Person Company instead of Sole Proprietor which provides many benefits like limited liability, corporate status, separate legal entity, independent existence, transferability, perpetual existence etc.

Frequently Asked Questions

Whether more than one person will be required for Proprietor registration ?

Only one person is required to start a Proprietorship and a Proprietorship can have only one owner.. As the name suggest, only one person is the owner of the Business and there is no requirement of any other Partner for Registration of Sole Proprietorship Firm.

What is the amount capital required to start Proprietorship Firm?

There is no minimum requirement of capital. Hence, it can be started with any amount.

What are the eligibility criteria to be a Proprietor?

The Proprietor must be an Indian citizen and a Resident of India. Non-Resident Indians and Persons of Indian Origin can only invest in a Proprietorship with prior approval of the Government of India.

Under which Government Authority, the Sole Proprietorship Firm Registration is registered?

In India, there is no specific law to regulate the Sole Proprietorship firms registration. It is an unregistered entity. TBS provides registration of Sole Proprietor under MSME (Micro, Small and Medium Establishments) Development Act, 2006 of Central Government.

What are the documents required for registration of Proprietorship Firm in India?

PAN Card for the Proprietor along with Adhaar card and address proof is sufficient to start a Proprietorship and obtain other registration, as applicable or required.

Will my Proprietorship firm receive a Certificate of Incorporation or Registration from Any Authority?

Proprietorship firms do not have a Certificate of Registration or Certificate of Incorporation. The identity and legitimacy of a Proprietorship firm is established by registering with the relevant or applicable Government authorities only.

How can I open a bank account for a Proprietorship?

To open the bank account in the name of Sole Proprietorship Firm, RBI mandates that the proprietor to provide two forms of registration for the Proprietorship along with the PAN Card, identity proof (Adhaar mandatory) and address proof of the Proprietor.

The two forms of registration can be any two of the following: Registration under Shop & Establishment Act, MSME registration, GST registration, Professional license, Chartered Accountant certificate or others as provided in the RBI Know Your Customer norms or the requirement of the respective Banks.

How to register the name of a Proprietorship?

There is no registry or regulation for the registering the name of a Proprietorship. Therefore, proprietorship firms can adopt any name that do not infringe on registered trademarks. Since there are no registry or regulation for registering the name of a Proprietorship, the only way to ensure exclusive use of the business name is to obtain a Trademark Registration of the business name.

Will my Proprietorship firm have a separate legal identity or separate PAN card?

No, There is no difference between the Proprietorship firm and the Proprietor. The PAN Card of the Proprietor will be the PAN Card of the Proprietorship business only. Therefore, there will be no separate legal identity for the business. The assets and liabilities of the Proprietorship business and the Proprietor will also be one and the same.

What are the advantages of Registration for Proprietorship Firm under MSMED Act?

The registered entity under MSMED Act can get subsidies, incentives and schemes launched by the Central Government in respect of the specific Business on the basis of registration certificate. Know more about the advantages do call our Advisors.

Which kinds of business can be registered under MSMED Act?

Any kind of business can apply for this registration. However, presently, Central Government has excluded the activities of Trading from registration under this Act. The business with Trading Activities can apply for registration under Shop & Establishments Act at tejbusinessservices link.

Do I require GST Registration for my Sole Proprietorship Firm?

There are certain criteria and if the any business entity including the Proprietary firm falls within the Criteria then it is mandatory to get registration under GST;

  • An aggregate turnover exceeding Rs. 20 lakh in a financial year mandates registration for a dealer or service provider under this law. However, for dealers conducting business in north-eastern states of India, an aggregate turnover exceeding Rs. 10 lakh mandates a registration
  • Any online platform operator.
  • Firm engaged in inter-state supply of Goods or Services.
Can I appoint new partners in a Proprietorship?

As its name suggests, the Sole Proprietorship Firms cannot have any other Business Partner as is is owned and controlled by one person only. If your Business require involvement of Partners, you may opt for conversion of Proprietorship to Private Limited Company or Limited Liability Partnership, as the case may be.

Can other people invest in a Proprietorship?

Proprietorship firms are business entity that are owned, managed and controlled by one person. So, Proprietorship firms cannot allows investors to invest therein.

What are the Annual compliance requirements for Sole Propritorship Firm in India?

Proprietorship will have to file their annual tax return with the Income Tax Department. Other tax filings such as GST returns filing may be necessary from time to time, based on the business activity performed and registrations opted.

Can the Proprietorship firm be converted into a Company or LLP?

A proprietorship can be taken over by the company or LLP.

Whether the audit is mandatory for a Proprietorship?

It is not necessary for Proprietorships to prepare audited financial statements each year. However, a tax audit may be necessary based on turnover and other criterion.

How will TBS help me start a Proprietorship?

An TBS Associate will understand your business requirements and help you start a Proprietorship by obtaining the relevant registrations. TBS will help obtain the necessary registrations to help the Proprietor open a bank account in the name of the business, thereby proving an identity for the business.

List of Documents Required for Proprietorship Firm registration ?

  • PAN (Permanent Account Number) of all proposed directors / Share Holders
  • Address Proof (Voter Id, Passport, Driving License, Aadhar Card) of all proposed Directors / ShareHolders.
  • Latest passport size Photographs of all directors / Share Holders.
  • Registered Office Address Proof - Electricity Bill/Mobile Bill/Gas Bill/Utility Bill along with Rent Agreement OR Ownership proof OR NOC
  • Additional Address proof all directors / promoters with Present address Mobile bill OR Telephone bill OR Electricity bill OR Bank Statement
